APP NAME: MemoryOS (AI Personal Assistant for Everything on Your Phone)

CORE IDEA:
Save everything (notes, screenshots, files, tasks, calendar events, clipboard items) into one AI-powered memory system. Users can search naturally or ask questions and instantly find information.

DESIGN STYLE:
- Minimal and modern
- Apple Notes simplicity
- Google Photos search experience
- ChatGPT conversational interface
- Notion-style cards
- Rounded corners (16-24px)
- Soft shadows
- Large search-first UI
- Bottom navigation
- Light & Dark mode
